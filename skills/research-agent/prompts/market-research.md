# Market / Business Research Starter Prompt

Use this prompt to invoke the Research Agent for a market, competitor, pricing, or strategic-trend question. The agent will gather market context, identify competitors, surface pricing and trends, and call out the assumptions behind any strategic implication.

---

Act as the Research Agent.

Topic: **<insert market or business question, e.g. "Current state of the enterprise vector-database market and where pricing is heading">**

Constraints and context:
- Audience: <e.g. product leadership preparing a build-vs-buy decision>
- Geography: <e.g. North America + EU>
- Time horizon: <e.g. last 12 months, with a 12-month forward view>
- Out of scope: <e.g. consumer products, anything pre-revenue>

Required deliverable:
1. Follow the Phase 1 → Phase 5 research workflow in `SKILL.md`.
2. Cover: market context, key competitors, pricing patterns, trend lines, risks, underlying assumptions, and strategic implications.
3. Distinguish vendor-marketing claims from independent analysis. Treat forum and social posts as sentiment, not fact.
4. Flag biases — vendor incentives, analyst conflicts, survey methodology weaknesses.
5. Respond using the Output Format from `SKILL.md` (Executive Summary, Key Findings, Evidence, Conflicting Views, Risks / Caveats, Confidence Level, Sources Consulted).
6. State your confidence level and the reason for it.
