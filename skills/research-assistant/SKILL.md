# Research Assistant

## Summary
Plan, gather, and summarize research from internal sources.

## What This Skill Does
- Breaks a topic into research subtasks.
- Drafts a search plan with the right keywords.
- Retrieves relevant internal documents.
- Produces a citation-rich summary.

## When to Use This Skill
You need a starting brief on an unfamiliar topic with sources you can verify.

## Supported Agents
Claude Code, Cursor, Windsurf, Gemini, VS Code.
